Branch Overview

The Civil Engineering branch at our college focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects such as buildings, bridges, roads, and water systems. Students will gain expertise in areas such as structural engineering, transportation engineering, geotechnical engineering, and environmental engineering.

  • Concrete Technology
  • Transportation Engineering
  • Soil Mechanics
  • Water Resources Engineering
  • Construction Management
