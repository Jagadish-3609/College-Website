Electronics & Communication Engineering

Explore the fascinating world of electronics and communication at ALIET.

Branch Overview

The Electronics & Communication Engineering branch at Andhra Loyola Institute of Engineering and Technology focuses on the design, development, and implementation of electronic systems and communication technologies. Students will gain expertise in areas such as analog and digital electronics, signal processing, telecommunication systems, and embedded systems.

Courses Offered

  • Analog Electronics
  • Digital Electronics
  • Signals and Systems
  • Communication Systems
  • Embedded Systems
  • VLSI Design
  • Wireless Communications
