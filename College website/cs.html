Branch Overview

The Computer Science branch at Andhra Loyola Institute of Engineering and Technology offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers the theoretical and practical aspects of computing. Students will gain a deep understanding of computer hardware, software, algorithms, and programming languages, enabling them to develop innovative solutions to complex problems.

  • Operating Systems
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Computer Networks
  • Database Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
