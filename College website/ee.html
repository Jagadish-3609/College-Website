Electrical Engineering

Harness the power of electricity with our Electrical Engineering branch.

Branch Overview

The Electrical Engineering branch at our college equips students with the knowledge and skills required to design, develop, and maintain electrical systems and components. Students will gain expertise in areas such as power systems, control systems, electrical machines, and renewable energy technologies.

Courses Offered

  • Electrical Circuit Analysis
  • Electrical Machines
  • Power Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Renewable Energy Technologies
  • Electric Drives
  • Power Electronics
